# Malaga Marathon — Strength, Core, Flexibility & Yoga Plan

Supplementary plan layered onto the 12-week Pfitzinger Malaga build (Plan 1, start 2026-08-17, race 2026-11-08). Mon rest; Tue–Sun running, 5–6 sessions/week, ~95–122 km.

Athlete priority: **injury prevention** (history of injury from strength deficit) and **maintaining upper-body muscle / aesthetics**.

---

## Scheduling logic

1. **Consolidate hard stress.** Strength goes on days that are already hard (quality runs), not on recovery days — keeps easy days genuinely easy. Run AM / lift PM (6+ hr gap) to honour Pfitz's "not right before or after a hard run." Single session that day → lift *after* the run, never before.
2. **Protect the pillars.** Sunday long run + Tue/Fri quality sessions are sacred: flexibility before, static stretch after, nothing that pre-fatigues going in.
3. **Strength = maintenance, not hypertrophy.** Lower-body work is injury-proofing (single-leg, posterior chain, glute-med, eccentric calf). Upper-body work is muscle *retention* — driven by intensity (load), not volume.

---

## Weekly template (anchor pattern)

Typical week: Tue = speed/hills, Wed = MLR, Thu = recovery, Fri = LT, Sat = recovery, Sun = long/MP.

| Day | Run | Supplementary work |
|-----|-----|---------------------|
| **Mon** | REST | Mobility/yoga flow + **Core A** + longer static stretch routine |
| **Tue** | Quality (hills/strides) | Dynamic warm-up before · **Strength A** in the evening |
| **Wed** | MLR | Dynamic warm-up before · static stretch after |
| **Thu** | Recovery | **Core B** (after run) |
| **Fri** | LT / GA | Dynamic warm-up before · **Strength B** in the evening |
| **Sat** | Recovery | **Core C** (after run) + static stretches |
| **Sun** | Long / MP | Full dynamic warm-up before · static stretch after |

**Rule, not the days:** glue strength to that week's two *quality* days, core to the *easy/rest* days. Per-week map at bottom.

## Strength A — lower + upper *push* (~40 min)

Lower block as a circuit ×2; upper lifts as straight sets, heavier.

| Block | Exercise | Prescription |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Lower | Step-up with knee raise | 8 / leg ×2 |
| Lower | Single-leg RDL | 8 / leg ×2 |
| Lower | Squat | 12 ×2 |
| Lower | Calf raise (3s eccentric) | 12 ×2 |
| Upper | Chest press (DB/barbell) | 3 × 6–10, RPE 8 |
| Upper | Overhead press | 3 × 6–10, RPE 8 |
| Upper | Pull-up | 3 × AMRAP |
| Core | Side plank | 5 / side, 20s |

## Strength B — lower + upper *pull* (~40 min)

| Block | Exercise | Prescription |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Lower | Reverse lunge | 12 / leg ×2 |
| Lower | Single-leg glute bridge | 10 / leg ×2 |
| Lower | Clamshell / mini-band step | 12 / side ×2 |
| Lower | Single-leg calf raise | 12 / leg ×2 |
| Upper | Pull-up | 3 × AMRAP |
| Upper | Bicep curl | 3 × 8–12 |
| Upper | Chest press *or* overhead press (rotate) | 3 × 6–10 |
| Core | Bird dog | 12 / side |

Weekly frequency: chest press ~2×, OHP ~1.5–2×, pull-up 2×, curls 1–2×.

**Muscle-retention rules:** lift heavy, stop 1–2 reps short of failure (RPE 8); protein 1.6–2.2 g/kg/day; do not add volume in peak weeks (4 & 7).

## Core sessions (Pfitz circuit, ~12 min, 3×/week)

Mon / Thu / Sat. Run twice through. Never inside a hard-run warm-up.

- Bird dog × 12 / side
- Leg push-away × 15 / side
- Single-leg glute bridge × 10 / side
- Side plank × 5 / side, 20s
- Bicycle crunch × 12 / side
- Prone plank with leg lift × 30s, lifting each leg 3×

## Dynamic flexibility / yoga — warm-up (before longer/harder runs)

Before Tue, Wed, Fri, Sun especially:
Cat-cow × 6 → leg swing × 12 / leg → side swing × 12 / leg → side lunge × 6 / side → walking knee hugs × 8 / leg → side skip × 12 each way.

## Static stretches — after runs / end of strength

Pick 3–4, 2–3 reps/side, 15–30s, targeting what the day loaded:
Bent-leg calf · straight-leg calf · lying hamstring · quad · hip flexor · glute · hip rotator · shoulder & lat.

## 12-week periodization

| Weeks | Phase | Strength | Core | Notes |
|-------|-------|----------|------|-------|
| 1–5 | Endurance | 2× full (A+B), progress load | 3× | Build the base — main injury-proofing window |
| 6–7 | Race prep / peak vol | 2×, hold load | 3× | Weeks 4 & 7 highest km — don't chase gym PRs |
| 8 | Tune-up race (Sat) | 1× (Tue only), lighter | 2× | Drop Strength B; Sat race = quality day |
| 9 | Final big block | 2×, hold | 3× | Last full-strength week |
| 10 | Taper + race (Sat) | 1× (Tue only), light | 2× | Maintenance loads only |
| 11 | Sharpening taper | 1× light (upper-body kept, 2×8) | 2× light | Legs taper; upper-body retention continues |
| 12 | Race week | none (mobility/yoga only) | core ×1 (Mon, light) | Nothing that makes you sore |

Last hard *leg* strength = end of Week 9. Upper-body lifting (lighter) continues through Week 11 — it doesn't fatigue the legs and holds race-day shape.

## Week-specific flags

- **Weeks 8 & 10** (Sat tune-up races): strength → Tuesday only; Thu/Fri genuinely easy.
- **Week 11** (VO₂ on Thu): move Strength B off Friday if legs are heavy.
- **Week 12**: zero loading; Mon light core + mobility/yoga; dynamic warm-ups before shakeouts only.
